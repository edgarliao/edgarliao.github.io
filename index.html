How This Works

The First-ever Alumni-for-Students Crowdfunding Portal -You pledge an amount, we charge your donation to your membership account and handle the transaction for you.

Ease and Convenience

You pledge and we take care of the rest.

Quality and Impact

We carefully select and curate the projects that are featured in our campaigns and monitor their progress to ensure that your donations are spent wisely and efficiously

Safety and Security

There is little risk - you are charged as per normal and the Society handles the transfer of funds to the project you support through our stringent financial systems.

About Us

This is a NUSS Initiative, in collaboration with NUS ASAC, NUS OAR and NUS DVO.

The National University of Singapore Society

Singapore's oldest graduate society, founded in 1954 to serve graduates, university, and society

The NUS Alumni Student Advancement Committee

A committtee of senior and young alumni who seek to mobilize alumni to support NUS student advancement in collaboration with the NUS Office of Alumni Relations and the NUS Office of Development

The National University of Singapore

Singapore's oldest university, founded in 1905
